Top 3 favourite Christopher Nolan's movies

In no particular order

Oppenheimer

Oppenheimer is a movie that brings you back to the 1940s where the journey of making the first atomic bomb begins. The movie was based on the book "American Prometheus". Every scene or conversation will amaze you with the sheer magnitude of each one.

Prestige

Prestige is a movie of magicians trying to be the best one out there but with no limitations. What makes this movie great was the twists. It leaves you with not being able to expect what comes next and also leaves you bamboozled with how the movie unfolds right till the end.
